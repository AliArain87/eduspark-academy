About Us

EduSpark Academy is a premier tuition center in Hyderabad, Pakistan, dedicated to igniting a passion for learning among students from nursery to 10th class. Established in 2025, we offer expert tutoring in subjects including Computer Science, Mathematics, Biology, English, Islamiyat, Pakistan Studies, Physics, and more. Our mission is to empower every student with the knowledge and skills to excel academically and beyond.

Our teaching philosophy revolves around small class sizes, experienced educators, and a student-centered approach. We believe in fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and confidence, ensuring each child reaches their full potential. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, we are committed to serving our community with quality education tailored to individual needs.
